I am Md Saydur Rahman currently a Ph.D. student in Electrical Engineering at the University of California Riverside (UCR), and I am expected to graduate in 2025. Currently, I am working as a Research Assistant under the supervision of Dr. Yingbo Hua. My research interest involves Wireless Physical Layer Security, 5g Low-latency Communication, Domain adaptation-based on Deep learning, and Federated learning.
